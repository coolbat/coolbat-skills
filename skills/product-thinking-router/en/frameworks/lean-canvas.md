# Lean Canvas

## What problem it solves
Lean Canvas structures an early product idea into a testable business and product hypothesis.

## Best for
- 0→1 product definition
- startup or new-product clarification
- assumption discovery

## Not for
- detailed product specification
- backlog prioritization

## Use when
- the idea is promising but still fuzzy
- the team needs one-page strategic clarity

## Core sections
- Problem
- Customer segments
- Unique value proposition
- Solution
- Channels
- Revenue
- Cost
- Key metrics
- Unfair advantage

## Output template
Problem: ______
Target users: ______
Current alternatives: ______
Unique value proposition: ______
Solution: ______
Key assumptions: ______

## Common misuse
Do not treat Lean Canvas as proof.
It is a hypothesis map, not evidence.
