# RICE

## What problem it solves
RICE helps compare multiple opportunities using a structured prioritization method.

## Best for
- feature prioritization
- roadmap sequencing
- rational tradeoff decisions

## Not for
- discovering what users truly need
- understanding satisfaction type

## Use when
- there are multiple candidate initiatives
- the team needs a defensible prioritization logic

## Components
- Reach
- Impact
- Confidence
- Effort

## Output template
Initiative: ______
Reach: ______
Impact: ______
Confidence: ______
Effort: ______
RICE score: ______

## Common misuse
Fake precision is dangerous.
If reach or impact is pure guesswork, say so clearly and downgrade confidence.
