# 5 Whys

## What problem it solves
5 Whys helps move from a visible symptom toward a more plausible root cause.

## Best for
- post-launch analysis
- issue diagnosis
- breaking superficial explanations

## Not for
- statistical proof
- broad strategic planning by itself

## Use when
- the team only has a symptom statement
- people are jumping to conclusions too early
- you need a disciplined root-cause path

## Output template
Symptom: ______
Why 1: ______
Why 2: ______
Why 3: ______
Why 4: ______
Why 5: ______
Likely root cause: ______

## Common misuse
5 Whys can drift into storytelling.
Anchor each “why” to actual evidence when possible.
