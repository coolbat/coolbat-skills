# North Star Metric

## What problem it solves
North Star Metric aligns the team around the core value the product delivers to users.

## Best for
- top-level product success alignment
- avoiding fragmented metrics
- connecting user value to business growth

## Not for
- replacing all operational metrics
- acting as the only number the team ever sees

## Use when
- the team has too many success definitions
- the product lacks a clear value metric
- you want a stable product-wide direction

## Output template
Core user value: ______
North Star Metric: ______
Why it represents value: ______
Input metrics:
1. ______
2. ______
3. ______

## Common misuse
Do not choose a vanity metric just because it is large or easy to report.
A North Star should reflect delivered user value, not just activity volume.
