# Assumption Tree

## What problem it solves
Assumption Tree structures the uncertainties behind a product decision and highlights what must be validated first.

## Best for
- hypothesis planning
- 0→1 idea validation
- sequencing validation work

## Not for
- feature scoring after the market is already understood
- retrospective diagnosis of shipped work by itself

## Use when
- the idea depends on multiple unproven beliefs
- you need to know what to validate first
- the team confuses opinions with validated facts

## Output template
Decision / idea: ______
Key assumption: ______
Why it matters: ______
Risk if false: ______
How to test: ______
Priority to validate: ______

## Common misuse
Do not list assumptions without ranking them.
The point is to find the highest-risk unknowns first.
