# AARRR

## What problem it solves
AARRR helps diagnose growth problems across the user funnel.

## Best for
- growth bottleneck analysis
- funnel diagnosis
- lifecycle optimization

## Not for
- detailed UX diagnosis on its own
- prioritizing roadmap items without context

## Use when
- growth is slowing
- users drop off between stages
- acquisition is strong but retention or revenue is weak

## Funnel
- Acquisition
- Activation
- Retention
- Referral
- Revenue

## Output template
Current weak stage: ______
Evidence: ______
Possible cause: ______
Next hypothesis: ______
Next experiment: ______

## Common misuse
Do not assume the problem is always acquisition.
Many products have acquisition growth but activation or retention failure.
