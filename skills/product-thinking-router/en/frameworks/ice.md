# ICE

## What problem it solves
ICE provides a fast, rough way to prioritize ideas when evidence is incomplete.

## Best for
- early-stage backlog triage
- experiments
- rapid comparison under uncertainty

## Not for
- high-stakes roadmap decisions requiring detailed modeling
- final investment decisions when strong data exists

## Use when
- you need a quick prioritization pass
- most ideas are still assumptions
- the team is moving fast and needs a lightweight filter

## Components
- Impact
- Confidence
- Ease

## Output template
Idea: ______
Impact: ______
Confidence: ______
Ease: ______
ICE score: ______

## Common misuse
ICE is deliberately rough.
Do not present it as more rigorous than it is.
