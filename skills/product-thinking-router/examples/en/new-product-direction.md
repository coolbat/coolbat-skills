# Example: New Product Direction Is Fuzzy

## Problem Diagnosis
This is a strategy + discovery problem.

## Recommended Frameworks
- Lean Canvas
- Assumption Tree
- JTBD

## Why These Frameworks
- Lean Canvas structures the idea into a one-page hypothesis.
- Assumption Tree identifies what must be validated first.
- JTBD clarifies the user progress the product is meant to unlock.

## Apply to Current Question
Answer:
1. What core problem exists?
2. Who has that problem?
3. What progress are they trying to make?
4. What assumptions must be true for this product to work?
5. Which assumption is most dangerous if false?
